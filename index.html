Annika Santor

Composer, Arranger, and Vocalist

Bio

Annika Santor is a classically trained vocalist who has recieved multiple awards for her performances. She is also a composer and arranger, specializing in video game muisc and other types of soundtracks. She is currently studying at Berklee College of Music majoring in Game and Interactive Media Scoring with minors in Creative Coding and Classical Music. While at Berklee, she became co-music director of the Audire Soundtrack Collective's choir.

Skills

  • Composition for small ensembles
  • Composition for full orchestra and choir
  • Arranging for choir
  • Arranging for combined orchestra and choir
  • Performing classical and musical theater style vocals
